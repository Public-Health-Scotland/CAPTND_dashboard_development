For the quarter ending November 2025, there were a total of 7,607 individuals referred to PT recorded in CAPTND across NHSScotland. This was a decrease of 533 (6.5%) compared with the previous quarter, and an increase of 4,621 (154.8%) compared with the same quarter ending November 2024.

For some health boards referral numbers may appear to have increased sharply over the past five quarters, which may be reflective of improved data quality of submissions allowing more pathways to be counted in CAPTND, rather than necessarily representing an actual increase in service demand.

CAPTND data are still under development therefore any inferences or conclusions from this analysis must be treated with caution. Note that referral numbers represent those patients for whom it was possible to construct a pathway in CAPTND, and may therefore be under-representative of total referrals at health board level.
